semblance

Semblance is a website focused on local community-building by using technology to facilitate authentic real life interactions to avoid the substitution of tech for reality.

Connect with your local community

The internet allows us to connect with people all over the world but how often do you connect with people in your local community? Our goal is provide a safe platform where people can meet other people closeby with similar interests. Expand your local community today!

Develop authentic relationships

When creating Semblance, we wanted to create an experience that connected people based on our unique Archetype technology. Our unique system allows people to express their personality and connect with people who have similar interests.

Archetypes

We created a unique way for our users to match and search for other people! Archetypes serve as a representation of our personality types and allow people to match on a deeper level.

Inspiration

Thomas had the idea for Semblance while talking with a college roommate who wanted to meet a fictional character in real life.

"Well you can't meet them, but what if you could meet someone like them?"

To avoid liabilities with character IPs and to avoid building a massive character database, fictional characters were simplified to their story-telling roots: archetypes.

This is a project for fun and profit, but we will never sell nor publicly expose your personal information.
